Our Story

From a tiny kitchen to your favourite scoop — every batch is made with heart, soul, and the finest ingredients on earth.

Where It All Began

Igloo was born in 2018, inside a cramped kitchen with a second-hand ice cream maker and a wild dream: to create ice cream that tastes like a warm hug on a summer day.

What started as weekend experiments for friends and family quickly turned into a local sensation. Word spread — not through ads, but through genuine love for every single scoop.

Today, Igloo serves thousands of happy customers, but we've never lost that small-batch spirit. Every flavour is still developed by hand, tested obsessively, and made with the same passion as day one.

What We Stand For

All Natural

Zero artificial colours, zero preservatives. Just real, honest ingredients you can taste in every bite.

Made With Love

Every batch is small-batch churned by people who genuinely care about what ends up in your bowl.

Locally Sourced

We partner with local farms and suppliers to bring you the freshest dairy, fruits, and flavours.

Our Ingredients Promise

We believe that extraordinary ice cream starts with extraordinary ingredients. That's why we're obsessive about sourcing only the best:

  • Farm-fresh whole milk & cream from local dairies
  • Real Madagascar vanilla beans — never extract
  • Single-origin Belgian chocolate
  • Seasonal fruits picked at peak ripeness
  • Organic cane sugar — no corn syrup, ever
